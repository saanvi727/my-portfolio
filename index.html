About Me

I'm currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science at Cornell University, with a minor in Applied Economics and AI. I’ve interned at Hedgit Solutions building automated trading bots using TypeScript and Solana APIs, and I’ve worked on sentiment analysis projects using Python NLP tools.

As Vice President of Recruitment at Cornell FinTech Club, I actively contribute to AI-based financial fraud detection research. I’m also the founder of CompSci Network — a community initiative helping IB students and aspiring women in tech across the globe.

I'm especially interested in AI and finance — and I love working at the intersection of technology and social impact.
